## What Was Built

**Workspace Engine**
The structural layer. Every Workspace answers the same five questions in the same language.

**Signal Engine**
The single source of truth for every business signal. One write path. One archive. Permanent.

**Signal Registry**
25 signal types. Tier, severity, confidence, visibility, deduplication rules. The registry is the authority — never overridden at runtime.

**Signal Contract**
Every signal that enters the engine carries the same 20+ fields. No signal exists without evidence. No signal exists without a resolution condition.

**Signal Producers**
Five entity resolvers. Every business mutation publishes to the Signal Engine. Deal, Contact, Company, Task, Note. The Decision Resolver is untouched.

**Signal Archive**
Every signal ever produced is preserved. The archive is permanent. It is the historical record of every condition that crossed the threshold of consequence. It is the seed of future intelligence.

**Signal Scan Engine**
Five scanners. Temporal intelligence. Conditions that can only emerge between mutations — deal gone stale, relationship grown cold, task drifted past its deadline — are now detected. The engine runs on schedule. The archive fills regardless of user activity.

**Signal Query Layer**
Today reads from the Signal Archive. Nothing else. Business intelligence is produced once, in the engine, and consumed by any surface that needs it.

**Today v2**
The first surface fully powered by the Signal Engine. Zero business logic in the presentation layer. Signals in, decisions out.

**Health Monitoring**
Every workspace has a live view of its signal state. The scan schedule is visible. Failures are recorded. The system is observable.

**Lifecycle Verification**
The full signal lifecycle — produce, claim, dismiss, restore, resolve, expire — is verifiable end-to-end, on demand, against a live workspace.
